The age of digital money



If money today is digital, how does it work? A file represents a dollar. If that so it can copy million times and have million dollars. This is called "Double spend problem". The solution used is centralized on this ledger in the computer.



Invention of Bitcoin?



2008, Satoshi Nakamoto gave a solution without a centralizing. He called it as "Bitcoin" which is a decentralized solution.







What is Bitcoin?



Simply Bitcoin is "The first decentralized digital currency". In bitcoin, the coins (or rather the transactions) are all recorded in a ledger. This ledger is public and shared and it is maintained by the public. In Bitcoin, instead of accounts, money is moved between addresses like emails. This has very high security and it is difficult to hack because it stored as a "Blockchain".



Who accepts Bitcoin?



Businesses have started accepting it all around the world. There are web sites help you find Bitcoin accepting businesses. Also can used to book hotels on Expedia, shop for furniture on Overstock and buy Xbox games. Many marketplaces called "bitcoin exchanges" allow people to buy or sell bitcoins using different currencies.

Owning Bitcoins



Bitcoins are stored in a "digital wallet", which exists either in the cloud or on a user's computer. The wallet is a kind of virtual bank account that allows users to send or receive bitcoins, pay for goods or save their money.

Terminologies



Bid - Price a trader is willing to purchase a bitcoin

Ask - Price a seller is willing to sell a bitcoin

Last Price - Latest market value of a bitcoin

Spread - Difference between ask and bid as a percentage of ask

High – Highest value of a bitcoin during a given time period

Low – Lowest value of a bitcoin during a given time period

Open – Starting value of a bitcoin during a given period

Close – Ending value of a bitcoin during a given period
